Origins and purpose of the Fair Score Framework.

Background

The Fair Score Framework is based on academic research examining value chain fairness. It was developed to provide a practical, structured tool for assessing fairness in real‑world contexts.

The criteria used in the Fair Score Framework were developed in research deposited at:

  • University of Gloucestershire Research Repository
  • HAL Research Repository B

The research also informed a peer‑reviewed journal article and a chapter in an academic book:

  • Peer‑reviewed journal article
  • Academic book chapter
