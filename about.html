About

Origins and purpose of the Fair Score Framework.

Background

The Fair Score Framework is based on academic research examining fairness in organisational value chains. It was developed to provide a practical, structured tool for assessing fairness in real‑world contexts.

Research Foundation

The conceptual model is grounded in research deposited at HAL: hal‑05577901.
